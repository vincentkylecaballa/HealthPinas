About Us

About the Project

HealthPinas is an online web application that tracks, inform, and help the Filipino people in fighting the COVID – 19 pandemic in the country. This application will have several features, including an online tracker for available hospitals and isolation center around the NCR+ bubble, in which it is the epicenter of the virus.
The team’s intended audience is the people who are inside the NCR+ bubble (Bulacan, Cavite, Rizal, Metro Manila, Laguna), which is the epicenter of the virus in the country with most of the positive cases are coming from them. In the long run, we will add different places in the Philippines, in which the group intends to make the Filipino lives better
